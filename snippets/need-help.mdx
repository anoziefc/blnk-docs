## Need help?

We are very happy to help you make the most of Blnk, regardless of whether it is your first time or you are switching from another tool.

To ask questions or discuss issues, please [contact us](mailto:support@blnkfinance.com) or [join our Discord community](https://discord.gg/7WNv94zPpx). 

Love building with Blnk? Share your project with us and join our mailing list (optional) to stay in the loop with helpful tips, events, upcoming features and highlights from our open-source community. [Tell us here](https://www.blnkfinance.com/core/share-project).
